Who We Are

HY HCI Group

HY HCI Group is a research group at Hanyang University focused on Human-Computer Interaction and AI. We bring together researchers across multiple disciplines to understand how people interact with intelligent systems — and to design those systems in ways that are intuitive, transparent, and genuinely useful.

Our work spans foundational HCI research, AI safety, immersive experience, and digital health, grounded in rigorous empirical methods and published at top-tier venues in HCI and AI.

Our Vision

Shaping the Future of Human-AI Collaboration

We envision a world where AI amplifies human capability rather than replacing it — where people are empowered by intelligent systems that understand context, communicate their reasoning, and adapt to individual needs. HY HCI Group pursues this vision through rigorous research that puts human experience at the center of AI design.

Our Mission

Discover, Design, Disseminate

Our mission is to advance knowledge at the intersection of HCI and AI — discovering new insights about human behavior, designing systems that reflect those insights, and disseminating our findings to the broader research community.

Discover

Conduct rigorous empirical research to deepen our understanding of how people perceive, interact with, and are affected by AI systems.

Design

Build novel interactive systems and AI-driven tools grounded in human-centered principles, evaluated through iterative user studies.

Disseminate

Share our findings through publications at premier HCI and AI venues, open-source releases, and collaborative engagement with the research community.
