Immersive Experience

VR/AR/MR Spatial Computing Embodied Interaction Presence

We design and evaluate immersive environments using VR, AR, and mixed reality to enhance training, collaboration, and situated experiences. Our research investigates how spatial interaction and embodied feedback shape user presence, performance, and well-being.

Human-Robot Interaction

Collaborative Robots Teleoperation Intent Recognition Safety

Our work explores intuitive interfaces and communication protocols that enable safe, efficient collaboration between humans and robots. We investigate shared autonomy, real-time intent recognition, and multimodal feedback to reduce operator workload and improve task performance in both industrial and service robot settings.

Creativity Support Tools

Generative AI Co-creation Ideation Design Tools

We build and evaluate AI-powered tools that augment human creativity, reducing friction in ideation while preserving the designer's agency. Our research spans generative co-creation, mixed-initiative design systems, and longitudinal studies of how intelligent tools shape creative practice across domains such as visual design, writing, and engineering problem-solving.

Personalization AI

User Modeling Adaptive Interfaces Recommendation Context-awareness

We investigate how AI can learn individual user preferences and dynamically adapt interfaces and workflows to fit each person. Our research combines behavioral modeling, implicit feedback signals, and privacy-preserving learning to build systems that personalize at scale without compromising user autonomy or data security.

AI Safety

Alignment Robustness Fairness & Bias Adversarial Attacks

We investigate the reliability, fairness, and alignment of AI systems deployed in high-stakes environments. Our work spans adversarial robustness, bias detection and mitigation, and human oversight mechanisms that keep AI behavior predictable and aligned with human values.

Digital Health Care

mHealth Wearables Patient Monitoring Clinical AI

We design and evaluate AI-driven health technologies that support patients, caregivers, and clinicians. Our research covers wearable sensing for continuous health monitoring, intelligent symptom tracking applications, and decision-support tools that augment clinical judgment while preserving human oversight.
